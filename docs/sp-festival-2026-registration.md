### Start-Up & Innovation Description *(max 100 words)*

ServiceSync is a mobile-first business management app built for Singapore's independent home service professionals — plumbers, electricians, air-con technicians, and handymen. We replace fragmented WhatsApp threads, handwritten invoices, and cash-only payments with a single, zero-commission platform. Tradesmen can manage bookings, generate professional PDF invoices, and collect PayNow QR payments — all in under 60 seconds per job. Unlike gig platforms that take 15–30% commission, ServiceSync charges a flat subscription and takes nothing from your earnings. It installs as a Progressive Web App with no app store download required, designed for how Singapore tradesmen actually work.

### How do visitors engage at the festival?

Visitors can try a live demo on a laptop at the booth — walking through the full ServiceSync workflow: creating a booking, generating a professional PDF invoice, and watching a PayNow QR code appear instantly on screen. They can also scan a QR code to install ServiceSync as a PWA directly on their own phone during the festival.

For the interactive angle: visitors can role-play as a "customer" making a booking and watch how a technician would generate an invoice and collect payment in real time.

## SECTION 2 — Your Future Maker Story

### Describe your startup, the future you are building, and the impact you hope to create *(max 200 words)*

ServiceSync is building the future of dignified work for Singapore's 50,000+ independent home service professionals. These are the plumbers, electricians, air-con technicians, and handymen who keep Singapore running — but who are stuck managing their businesses through fragmented WhatsApp threads, handwritten invoices, and cash-only payments.

We believe the future of work is one where every skilled tradesperson, regardless of their digital literacy, can access professional business tools. ServiceSync puts a full business management suite in every technician's pocket: bookings, invoicing, and PayNow QR payment collection — all in under 60 seconds per job.

What makes this future meaningful is what we don't take: zero commission, zero WhatsApp API fees, zero barrier to entry. In a world where gig platforms increasingly commoditise skilled labour, we're building the opposite — tools that help tradesmen own their client relationships and keep every dollar they earn.

Our immediate future is a closed beta with Singapore tradesmen, co-designed with the people who will use it every day. The future we're building towards is one where a plumber in Jurong West has the same professional tools as a consulting firm in Marina Bay.

### In what ways is your innovation future-changing? *(max 150 words)*

ServiceSync changes the future in three concrete ways:

**Zero-commission economics.** Every existing platform takes 15–30% per transaction. ServiceSync charges a flat subscription, returning full earnings to the tradesperson — changing the financial future of Singapore's independent workforce.

**AI-assisted invoicing.** Our invoice generator uses job history and smart defaults to produce professional PDF invoices in seconds, bringing digital-native efficiency to an analogue industry.

**WhatsApp-free by design.** Instead of a $600–1,200/year WhatsApp Business API, we use free wa.me click-to-chat links — a deliberate architectural decision that saves every technician real money from day one.

**Multilingual and inclusive.** With English, Mandarin, and Malay support planned, we're building for Singapore's actual tradesperson population, not a Western tech demographic.

Together, these decisions redefine what a "business tool" means for the 50,000+ independent tradespeople who form Singapore's essential service backbone.

### Key message / takeaway

ServiceSync gives Singapore's tradesmen professional-grade business tools without taking a cut of what they earn — so every dollar of their skill stays in their pocket.

### What inspired you to start this venture?

I watched people in the trades struggle with the same problems repeatedly: WhatsApp threads that made it impossible to track jobs, invoices scribbled on paper that clients would lose, and cash payments with no record. Every existing solution was either designed for large contractors (too complex, too expensive) or gig platforms that extracted a commission in exchange for leads — trading one problem for another. I wanted to build something that put professional tools in the tradesperson's hands without taking anything away from them.

### What does your product do, who is it for, and what makes it different? *(max 80–100 words)*

ServiceSync helps Singapore's plumbers, electricians, air-con technicians, and handymen run their business from their phone. They can track bookings, send professional invoices, and collect PayNow payments — all in one place, in under a minute per job. It's built for independent tradesmen who are tired of juggling WhatsApp, paper receipts, and cash. What makes it different? We don't take a cut. No commissions, no platform fees on earnings — just a flat subscription and tools that actually work for the way tradesmen work.

### What are your next steps or goals?

Our immediate next step is a closed beta with 10–15 Singapore tradesmen across plumbing, electrical, and air-con services. We're measuring activation (first invoice within 14 days), weekly active usage, and net promoter score. From there, we're building referral partnerships with hardware stores and trade associations — distributing ServiceSync at the exact point where tradesmen already buy their tools. Longer term, we're developing GST-compliant invoicing automation and multilingual support in Mandarin and Malay to reach Singapore's full tradesperson population.
